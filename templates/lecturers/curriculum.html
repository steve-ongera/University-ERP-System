Course Description

This course covers advanced topics in computer science including algorithms, data structures, and computational theory. Students will develop practical skills through hands-on projects and theoretical understanding through rigorous analysis.

Learning Outcomes
  • Analyze and compare algorithm complexity
  • Design efficient data structures for specific problems
  • Apply computational theory to real-world scenarios
  • Develop optimized solutions for complex problems

Course Policies
Attendance

Students are expected to attend all lectures and tutorials. More than 3 unexcused absences may affect the participation grade.

Late Submissions

Assignments submitted late will be penalized 10% per day up to 3 days. After 3 days, no submissions will be accepted.

Academic Integrity

All work must be your own. Plagiarism or cheating will result in disciplinary action according to university policy.
